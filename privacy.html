Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024 — Last Updated: May 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Random Generator Pro (“we,” “us,” or “our”), accessible at randomgeneratorpro.online. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring complete transparency about how information is handled when you use our services. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights under applicable data protection laws.

Random Generator Pro is a suite of browser-based utility tools—including a cryptographic random number generator, a password architect, and a regional name picker. All random generation occurs entirely within your local browser environment using the crypto.getRandomValues() Web Crypto API. We operate on a strict zero-knowledge architecture: no generated output (numbers, passwords, names, or identities) is ever transmitted to, processed by, or stored on our servers.
