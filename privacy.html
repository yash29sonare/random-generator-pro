Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At Random Generator Pro, we strictly prioritize your privacy. We want to be completely transparent: we collect absolutely no personal data from you when you use our core generation tools. There are no user accounts, no tracking pixels logging your keystrokes, and no databases storing the random numbers, names, or passwords you generate.

All randomization happens locally on your browser. When you generate a secure password, pick a random name, or roll a number, the mathematical processing (such as the crypto.getRandomValues() function) executes directly on your local device's hardware. None of this data is ever transmitted to our servers or stored by us.

2. Cookies

While we do not use cookies to track your usage of our generation tools, our website may use standard cookies provided by third-party services. Specifically, we may use Google AdSense to display advertisements that help keep this utility free for everyone.

Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. Third Party Tools

In order to provide our service, we utilize a few third-party resources:

  • Tailwind CSS via CDN: Used for styling the website.
  • Google Fonts: Used to provide the brutalist typography (such as Archivo Narrow, Anton, and Geist).
  • Google AdSense: (As mentioned above) Used for displaying non-intrusive advertisements.

These third-party services may collect standard web log information (such as your IP address and user-agent string) when your browser requests their files. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these respective providers.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected directly on this page. Because we do not collect email addresses, we cannot notify you directly of changes, so we encourage you to review this page periodically.
